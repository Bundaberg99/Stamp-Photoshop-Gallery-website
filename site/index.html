Ceskoslovensko (1989): Tatra Rallye Pariz Dakar

Ceskoslovensko was a nation that split up to the present day Czech Republic and Slovakia. This stamp has Ceskoslovensko's truck (Tatra) that was participating in the Dakar-Paris Rally

USSR (1980): Space Race

This stamp was produced to celebrate the advancement of the Soviet Space Program during the historic space race up till 1980.

Equatorial Guinea (1976):

Equatorial Guinea released their stamps for motorcycle enthusiasts in 1976. This stamp depicts the two motorists K. Enders and R. Engelhardt.

Hungary (1975): Lunohod-1

Lunohod-1 is first of the 2 lunar rovers landed my the Soviet Union. This stamp was released in Hungary to celebrate the successful landing of the rover.

DPR Korea (1960): Benz, Type 300

The Type 300 was the best selling car in Korea back in the 60s, hence a series of stamps were released for this iconic car.

Hungary (1974): Mariner 4

This probe lauched by NASA on 28th November 1964, made its way to Mars by 15 July 1965 and took the first photo of another planet from space. Hungary made many stamps such as this one to remember and recognise the key celestial milsstones by human kind.

Equatorial Guinea (1971): Soyuz 11

Soyuz 11 was a crewed mission to board the world's first Space Station where they docked on 7th June 1971. This stamp series was made to commemorate the Soviet's successful mission.
